Menu Selection
Guests choose from a variety of food and beverage options available on the room service menu, including breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks, desserts, and beverages. Special dietary requests or preferences can be accommodated.
Payment
Guests typically settle the bill upon delivery or when checking out of the hotel. Payment methods may include cash, credit/debit cards, or charging the order to the guest's room account.
Service Etiquette
The room service attendant provides friendly and professional service to the guest, addressing any questions or concerns they may have. They may also offer to assist with setting up the meal or provide recommendations if requested.
Feedback
After the meal, the room service staff may ask the guest for feedback to ensure satisfaction with the food and service. This feedback helps the hotel improve its room service offerings and address any issues promptly.
Clean-up
Once the guest has finished dining, the room service attendant collects any empty dishes, utensils, and trays from the room, ensuring it is left clean and tidy.
